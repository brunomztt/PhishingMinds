# 🧠 Phishing Minds

> Human-focused cybersecurity platform that simulates phishing attacks to measure and improve employee security awareness.

---

## 🚨 Overview

**Phishing Minds** is a cybersecurity awareness platform designed to simulate real-world phishing attacks inside organizations.

The system allows companies to launch controlled phishing campaigns to test employee behavior, identify vulnerabilities, and improve their internal security culture.

Unlike traditional security tools that focus only on infrastructure, **Phishing Minds focuses on the human factor**, which is responsible for the majority of successful cyberattacks.

---

## 🎯 Main Goals

- Identify **which employees or departments are most vulnerable to phishing**
- Measure **real security awareness**
- Provide **data-driven insights for managers**
- Improve **organizational cybersecurity culture**

---

## 🏢 Multi-Company SaaS Platform

Phishing Minds was designed as a **multi-tenant SaaS platform**, meaning multiple companies can use the system independently.

Each company has:

- its own employees
- departments
- phishing campaigns
- dashboards
- statistics

---

## ✉️ Phishing Simulation Engine

The platform allows administrators to create and schedule phishing campaigns.

Features include:

- Custom phishing email templates
- Dynamic template parameters
- Campaign scheduling
- Department targeting
- Company-wide campaigns

Example phishing parameters:

```
{{EmployeeName}}
{{CompanyName}}
{{FakeLoginLink}}
{{ManagerName}}
```

---

## 📊 Behavioral Tracking

Phishing Minds tracks how users interact with phishing emails.

The system records events such as:

- 📬 Email opened  
- 🔗 Link clicked  
- 🔐 Credentials submitted  
- 🚩 Phishing reported  

These interactions are used to calculate **security risk metrics**.

---

## 🏆 Security Awareness Ranking

The platform generates rankings such as:

- Most secure departments
- Most vulnerable teams
- Users who frequently fall for phishing attacks

This creates a **gamified security awareness culture** within organizations.

---

## 📈 Executive Security Dashboard

Managers and executives have access to a **PowerBI-style dashboard** showing:

- phishing campaign results
- department risk levels
- user vulnerability statistics
- overall company security score

This helps leadership understand **human cybersecurity risk in real time**.

## 🔐 Why Phishing Minds?

Over **90% of cyberattacks start with social engineering**.

Phishing Minds helps organizations move from **reactive security** to **proactive human risk management**.

Instead of asking:

> *"Are our systems secure?"*

Companies can now ask:

> *"Are our people prepared?"*
